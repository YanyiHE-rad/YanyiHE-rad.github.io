Hi, I'm Chunlüe Zhou. My research mainly focues on observation data homogenization and regional climate change attribution.

Chunlüe Zhou is a young climatologist. His research interests cover daily data homogenization; extreme event attribution; regional climate variability&change; and ocean-land-atmos interaction. He's hosting an EU MSCA project. He's invited as expert reviewer for ~20 journals and reports, as IPCC AR6. Due to his excellent records, he's cited as Outstanding Reviewer for GRL and JMR, and won key awards, e.g., Baosteel Award. He's also keen on community services, e.g, as Editor for the journal ESSD.
